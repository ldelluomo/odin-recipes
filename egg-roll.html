Egg Roll Bowl

Description:

Craving egg rolls, but too lazy to actually wrap and fry them? You are in luck! Try out this Egg Roll Bowl recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Lean ground beef, 1 lb
  • Olive oil, 1 Tb
  • Sea salt, 1/2 tsp
  • Pepper, 1/4 tsp
  • Onion, one half
  • Carrot, julienned
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Cabbage, 3 cups
  • Ground ginger, 1 tsp
  • Soy sauce, 1/4 cup
  • Sesame oil, 2 tsp
  • Sugar, 1/2 tsp
  • Green onion, for garnish
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in skillet over med-high heat. Once hot, add beef and brown about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Add onion and carrots and saute 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and cook another 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
  3. Add cabbage, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar. Saute for 5-7 minutes until cabbage is tender.
  4. Serve garnished with green onions and sesame seeds.
