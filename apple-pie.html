Apple Pie

Description:

This delicious apple pie will quickly become a family favorite. Give it a try. I made this once for an office party and was harassed for weeks to share the recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Apples, 3 lbs
  • Lemon juice, 2 Tb
  • Sugar, 1 cup
  • All-purpose flour, 1/2 cup
  • Cinnamon, 1-1/2 tsp
  • Nutmeg, 1/4 tsp
  • Double-crust pie dough, 9-inch
  • Egg, lightly beaten

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. Toss apple slices with lemon juice. Add sugar, flour, and spices. Stir until apples are well coated.
  3. Roll one disc of pie dough into a 12-14 inch circle. Line a 9-inch pie pan with the dough. Pour apple fillng into crust.
  4. Roll second pie dough into an 11 inch circle. Place on top, trim excess dough, and crimp edges. Place on a rimmed baking sheet. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with sugar.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes. Reduce heat to 375 F and continue baking until crust is a deep golden brown, about 50 minutes to one hour. Let cool completely on a wire rack, about 4 hours.
