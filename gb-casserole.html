Green Bean Casserole

Description:

Why wait until Thanksgiving to enjoy this green bean casserole? Whether it's a lazy weeknight or you are just craving the taste of the holidays, whipping up this green bean casserole is quick and satisfying. Give it a try and let me know if it doesn't become an instant family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • Green beans, 2 lb
  • Kosher salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil, 2 Tbsp
  • Crimini mushrooms, sliced, 1 lb
  • Unsalted butter, 6 Tbsp
  • Thyme, 4 large sprigs
  • All purpose flour, 2 Tbsp
  • Whole milk, 1/4 cup
  • Heavy cream, 1 cup
  • Garlic cloves, finely grated, 4 cloves
  • Grated parmesan, 1/2 cup
  • Black pepper
  • French's fried onions, 3/4 cup

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F. Cook 2 lb. of green beans in large pot of salted boiling water for about 3 minutes. Drain and let cool. Trim stems and ends.
  2. Heat EVOO in large skillet over med-high heat. Cook 1/2 lb. mushrooms without stirring. Add 2 Tbsp. butter and 2 large thyme sprigs. Cook 4 mins. Season with kosher salt. Repeat with remaining butter, EVOO, mushrooms, and thyme sprigs.
  3. Melt remaining butter in saucepan. Whist in 2 Tbsp. flour and cook. Whisk in 1- 1/4 cup whole milk and 1 cup heavy cream. Remove from heat and whisk in garlic cloves, parmesan, and salt/pepper.
  4. Arrange green beans and mushrooms in baking dish. Pour sauce over mixture. Cover with foil and bake 25-30 minutes. Top with fried onions. Let sit 10 mins before serving.
